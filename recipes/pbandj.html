Description

We will be giving instructions on how to make a classic American staple, the Peanut butter and Jelly sandwich. With simple ingredients consisting of just, bread, peanut butter, and jelly. We'll really have our work cut out for us..

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Pick the two best pieces of bread you can find
  2. spread desired amount of peanut butter on the inside of both pieces of bread
  3. spread desired amount of jelly on top of peanut butter
  4. stick inside of both pieces of bread together
